Biography

I am a recent Ph.D. graduate from the College of Computer and Data Science at Fuzhou University, under the supervision of Prof. Min Gan. Before this, I earned both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Fuzhou University. Following my Master's degree, I spent three years working as an algorithm engineer at Imperial Vision Technology. My research interests are primarily in low-level computer vision and machine learning.
